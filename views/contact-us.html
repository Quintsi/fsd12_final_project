TaskMaster is a highly intuitive and robust to-do list application designed to streamline task management and enhance productivity for individuals and teams. Developed using the powerful Fatfree framework, TaskMaster offers a seamless user experience with a clean, responsive design suitable for all devices.

Due Date

The "Due Date" feature allows users to assign a specific completion date to each task on their to-do list. This helps in effectively managing deadlines and ensures that tasks are completed in a timely manner.

By setting a due date, users can organize their tasks more efficiently, prioritizing them based on the urgency of the deadlines.

Priority Levels

The "Priority Level" feature enables users to categorize tasks by their importance or urgency. Each task can be labeled as 'None,' 'Low,' or 'High' priority.

This classification assists in focusing on critical tasks that require immediate attention, thereby improving productivity and ensuring that important objectives are not overlooked in a busy schedule.
