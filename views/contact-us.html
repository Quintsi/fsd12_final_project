TaskMaster is a highly intuitive and robust to-do list application designed to streamline task management and enhance productivity for individuals and teams. Developed using the powerful Fatfree framework, TaskMaster Pro offers a seamless user experience with a clean, responsive design suitable for all devices.

Description Customization

Allows users to tailor task descriptions to their specific needs, enhancing clarity and personalization of task management.

Deadlines

Set explicit deadlines for tasks, helping users manage their time efficiently and stay on track with their objectives.

Priority Levels

Prioritize tasks with customizable levels, enabling users to focus on the most critical tasks at any given time.
